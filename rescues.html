Chewy is an excellent watch dog and a friendly companion. He enjoys walks, car rides, and chasing squirrels. He socializes well with people, cats, and dogs. Chewy has seasonal allergies which requires him to take medication with his food daily. He is trained to sit and shake hands. Chewy is now seven years old and neutered.

Solo has a big personality that you won't forget. He is a fantasic mouser as well as a handsome fellow. Solo socializes well with dogs but not with other cats. Solo is five years old and neutered. He has all of his claws intact. He is scared easily by young children, so it is best if they do not mix.

Adopt Solo

Spooky is a regal princess that will run your household as soon as she walks in. Spooky is extremely friendly with people and loves to be petted and snuggled. She enjoys playing with toys on strings and mellowing out on catnip. She loves people but does not interact well with dogs or other cats. Spooky is about ten years old, spayed, and has been declawed.

Adopt Spooky

Alfred is a gentle loving dog who enjoys spending time with people and going for walks. He is about 13 years old and is clinically blind. Alfred has adapted to navigate his environments using hearing and smell. He takes medications for arthritis daily with his food. He has been neutered. Therefore he requires lots of love, time, and attention.

Adopt Alfred
